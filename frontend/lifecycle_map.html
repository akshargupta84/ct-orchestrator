The positioning

CT Orchestrator doesn't replace the creative process — Strategy, Ideation, and Concept Development remain human-driven. Instead, it augments the stages where data and AI add the most value: generating test plans that feed in-lab evaluation, predicting creative performance before the expensive lift study, optimizing creatives in flight based on test outcomes, and discovering best practices that make every future brief smarter. The full loop connects both lifecycles: plans drive testing, test results drive optimization, and learnings feed the next creative brief.
