# Sample Testing Hypotheses

Use these when creating test plans. Copy/paste into the hypotheses field.

## Video Creative Hypotheses

Emotional storytelling will drive higher brand recall and awareness than product-focused messaging
Shorter 15s cutdowns will maintain brand lift impact while allowing for higher frequency
Product demonstration content will outperform lifestyle content for consideration
Authentic customer testimonials will increase credibility and trust metrics
Aspirational lifestyle content will resonate more strongly with younger demographics (18-34)
Educational how-to content will drive deeper engagement and longer view times
Humor-based creative will improve likability but may reduce message clarity
Celebrity endorsement will boost awareness but may not impact purchase intent

## Display Creative Hypotheses

Clean product photography will outperform lifestyle imagery for direct response
Promotional messaging with urgency will drive higher click-through rates
Native ad formats will feel less intrusive and generate higher engagement
Carousel formats showing multiple products will increase consideration
Larger ad formats (300x600, 970x250) will command more attention
Dynamic creative optimization will improve relevance scores
Retargeting creative with reminder messaging will drive higher conversion
Animation will increase attention but may decrease brand recall

## Cross-Channel Hypotheses

Consistent creative across channels will reinforce brand recognition
Channel-specific optimization will outperform one-size-fits-all approach
Sequential storytelling across touchpoints will improve consideration
Video exposure will amplify display ad effectiveness
Audio reinforcement will strengthen TV campaign message

## Audience-Specific Hypotheses

Personalized messaging will outperform generic creative for known customers
Acquisition creative should focus on category benefits vs brand benefits
Lapsed customer creative emphasizing "what's new" will drive reactivation
High-value customer creative can focus on premium/exclusive messaging
